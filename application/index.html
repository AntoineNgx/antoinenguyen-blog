Research Context

Foundation
Built on doctoral research examining how cyclical financial performance and top management team changes affect narrative strategy in annual report disclosures. Data: US biopharma 10-K filings (Item 7 MD&A), with financial data from S&P Global.
Key Signals
Cosine similarity (year-over-year text stability) · Sentiment analysis (LM finance dictionary) · Gunning Fog readability · TF-IDF topic shift · Abnormal change score cross-referenced with ROA and leadership events.
Scope
Portfolio demo — not an investment, legal, or academic regression product. Designed to show how unstructured corporate disclosure text can be transformed into decision signals.
